Our Process

Step 1: Applicant Registration

  1. Applicants visit The Working Lady website and fill out a registration form with their personal details, contact information, and job preferences.
  2. Upon successful registration, applicants receive a confirmation email with login credentials to access their dashboard.

Step 2: Recruiter Registration and Login

  1. Recruiters sign up by providing company information and contact details on The Working Lady platform.
  2. After registration, recruiters log in to their dashboard using their credentials.

Step 3: Recruiter Dashboard

  1. Recruiters access their dashboard, where they can post job listings with specific requirements, including job descriptions, qualifications, experience levels, and any other necessary details.
  2. Recruiters can also view applications submitted by applicants, review resumes, and shortlist candidates based on their criteria.

Step 4: TWL Connection

  1. The Working Lady platform facilitates connections between recruiters and applicants by notifying shortlisted candidates about job opportunities.
  2. Applicants receive notifications about job matches and can communicate with recruiters through the platform.

Step 5: Interview and Hiring

  1. Recruiters and applicants conduct interviews and assessments through TWL's messaging system or other preferred communication methods.
  2. Upon successful interviews, recruiters make hiring decisions and extend job offers to selected candidates.

Step 6: Onboarding and Support

  1. The Working Lady provides onboarding support to help newly hired candidates integrate smoothly into their roles.
  2. Recruiters and candidates can access ongoing support from TWL's customer service team for any assistance or inquiries.
